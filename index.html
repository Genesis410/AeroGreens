About AeroGreen

Our Mission

At AeroGreen, we're revolutionizing environmental monitoring by creating intelligent systems that don't just detect problems but actively work to solve them in real-time.

The Problem We Solve

In high-sensitivity spaces like libraries and hospitals, undetected spikes in sound pollution and toxic air particles disrupt health and concentration. Current solutions are either passive or disconnected from real-time user awareness.

Our Solution

AeroSentinel actively detects sound and air pollution thresholds, instantly alerts users via a sleek Blynk-powered dashboard, and activates intelligent fan purifiers. Unlike static sensors, it creates an interactive, live ecosystem for proactive environmental control.
